Introduction

Welcome to the NASA PACE Learning Hub! This guide is designed to help teachers effectively utilize the resources available on our website to enhance classroom learning about the PACE mission, which focuses on the interactions between oceans and the atmosphere and their impact on Earth's climate.

OBJECTIVES

By using this guide, teachers will be able to:

  • Integrate PACE mission resources into their curriculum.
  • Engage students with interactive learning materials.
  • Foster understanding of key concepts related to oceans, aerosols, and climate change.
